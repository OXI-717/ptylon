# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, constructive, and mindful that terminal logs and screenshots may
contain private data. Give and accept feedback in good faith.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, publishing private
information without permission, and other conduct that creates an unsafe or
unwelcoming environment are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior through the repository's private security advisory
channel. Project maintainers will review reports promptly and may remove,
reject, or restrict contributions when necessary.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
